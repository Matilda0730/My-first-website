eggdog

Eggdog is a computer-generated egg-shaped dog. The character has been used in a variety of remix videos, image macros and fan artworks.

Origin

On January 29th, 2019, Instagram user @groomer_andrea posted a video of herself grooming a Pomeranian dog named PomPom. The post received more than 97,000 views and 13,000 likes in less than two months (shown below).
